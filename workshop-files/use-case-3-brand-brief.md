# Brand Identity Brief — Aether Technologies

## Company Overview

**Company Name:** Aether Technologies
**Industry:** Manufacturing Technology / Supply Chain / AI
**Founded:** 2024, London
**Stage:** Seed

## What We Do

Aether Technologies builds AI-powered supply chain optimisation software for mid-market manufacturers. Our platform combines demand forecasting, supplier risk scoring, and inventory optimisation into a single dashboard — replacing the spreadsheets and gut-feel decisions that cost manufacturers millions in lost margin every year.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** COOs and Operations Directors at UK manufacturers with £10–100M revenue
- **Secondary:** Supply chain managers, procurement leads, and CFOs at the same companies
- **Industry verticals:** Food & beverage manufacturing, automotive components, consumer goods, industrial equipment

## Brand Personality

We want to feel:
- **Modern** — we're a tech company, not a legacy ERP vendor
- **Trustworthy** — manufacturers are risk-averse; we need to feel safe and credible
- **Technical but approachable** — smart without being intimidating; we simplify complexity
- **Confident** — we know our space and we deliver results

We do NOT want to feel:
- Cold or corporate
- Overly "startup-y" or gimmicky
- Generic SaaS / bland tech
- Intimidatingly complex

## Visual Direction

- **Colours we like:** Blues and teals — they feel professional and trustworthy. Open to suggestions for accent colours.
- **Style references:** Think Stripe's clarity, Notion's simplicity, or Palantir's sense of sophistication
- **Logo thoughts:** Something clean and geometric. The name "Aether" (the pure upper air of Greek mythology) could inspire flowing or atmospheric forms, but we don't want anything too abstract or hard to reproduce at small sizes.

## Competitive Landscape

Our brand needs to stand apart from:
- **Kinaxis** — established, enterprise-focused, feels corporate and heavy
- **o9 Solutions** — modern but very "big enterprise AI" in their branding
- **Blue Yonder** — well-known, traditional supply chain brand, recently modernised

We want to feel like the **fresh, modern alternative** — the company that mid-market manufacturers choose because the big players feel like overkill.

## Deliverables Needed

- Logo (icon + wordmark)
- Colour palette with hex codes
- Typography recommendations
- Basic brand guidelines (tone of voice, do's and don'ts)

## Tagline Ideas (Optional — Open to AI Suggestions)

- "Smarter supply chains, built for manufacturers."
- We're open to something better.
