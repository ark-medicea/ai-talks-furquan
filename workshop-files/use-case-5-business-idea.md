# Aether Technologies — Pitch Deck Brief

**Aether Technologies** is building AI-powered supply chain optimisation software for mid-market manufacturers (£10–100M revenue). The problem is clear: these manufacturers lose 15–20% of their margin to supply chain inefficiency — inaccurate demand forecasts, unreliable suppliers, and excess inventory — yet they're too small for enterprise solutions like Kinaxis or Blue Yonder and too complex for spreadsheets. Aether's platform combines real-time demand forecasting with supplier risk scoring and automated reorder recommendations, reducing waste, preventing stockouts, and improving margins by 8–12% within six months of deployment. The UK manufacturing software market is worth £2.1B and growing at 11% CAGR, with mid-market manufacturers chronically underserved by existing tools. Aether has three pilot customers (two food manufacturers, one automotive components supplier), £180K in ARR, and 94% gross retention after the first renewal cycle. The founding team combines deep manufacturing operations experience with machine learning expertise. We're raising a £1.5M seed round to hire our first sales team, expand the product to cover logistics optimisation, and scale from 3 to 30 customers within 18 months.
